I have recently defended my PhD in Computational Neuroscience at INRIA Rhône-Alpes in Lyon under the supervision of Hugues Berry within the BEAGLE Team. In my PhD research, I used mathematical modeling and computer simulation of subcellular signal transduction pathways to study the basis of learning in basal ganglia, the synaptic plasticity of basal ganglia neurons.
