I am a PhD candidate at INRIA Rhône-Alpes in Lyon. I am carrying out my research in the field of Computational Neuroscience under the supervision of Hugues Berry within the BEAGLE Team. My research focuses on mathematical modeling and simulation of signal transduction underlying the synaptic plasticity of basal ganglia neurons.
